ABOUT OUR KART

SMART
IGNITION SYSTEM

The kart is equipped with a mobile-enabled ignition system, aptly named the Mobile Start-Stop (MSS) module. This innovative technology allows the driver to remotely start and stop the kart using a designated mobile application. Integrated with the kart's engine control unit (ECU), the MSS module communicates wirelessly with the driver's smartphone, enabling convenient and efficient control over the vehicle's operation. With the MSS module, the driver gains enhanced flexibility and accessibility, streamlining the start-up and shutdown processes for optimal convenience and safety.

KART DRIVER
STAY ALERT SYSTEM (K-DAS)

The Kart Driver Alert System (K-DAS) is a safety feature designed to combat driver drowsiness in karting environments. Using EEG sensors, infrared cameras, and accelerometers, it monitors the driver's vital signs and steering inputs in real-time. When signs of fatigue are detected, such as drooping eyelids or erratic steering, the system triggers audible, visual, or haptic alerts to wake the driver and alert track officials if necessary. By proactively addressing drowsiness, the K-DAS enhances safety and prevents accidents on the karting track.

BLIND SPOT
DETECTION SYSTEM

It is a safety feature integrated into the kart, designed to enhance awareness and prevent accidents caused by unseen vehicles in adjacent lanes. Utilizing advanced sensors strategically positioned around the kart, the BSD continuously monitors blind spots and detects the presence of vehicles that may not be visible to the driver. When a vehicle is detected in the blind spot, the system activates visual and/or auditory alerts to warn the driver, prompting them to exercise caution before changing lanes or maneuvering the kart. This proactive safety measure significantly reduces the risk of collisions and enhances overall safety on the track.
