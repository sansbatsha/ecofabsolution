Advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) are primarily focused on collision avoidance technologies (for example, lane departure warning and blind-spot applications) and driver aids, such as night vision, driver alertness and adaptive cruise control.

TELEMATIC
CONTROL UNIT[TCU]

A telematic control unit (TCU) in the automobile industry is the embedded system on board a vehicle that wirelessly connects the vehicle to cloud services or other vehicles via V2X standards over a cellular network.It may also provide in-vehicle connectivity via Wifi and Bluetooth

Know more

CAN BUS
SYSTEM

A controller area network (CAN bus) is a vehicle bus standard designed to allow microcontrollers and devices to communicate with each other. It is a message-based protocol, designed originally for multiplex electrical wiring within automobiles to save on copper, but it can also be used in many other contexts.

INFOTAINMENT SYSTEM

What is SmartPlay infotainment system? It transforms your smartphone into a touch interface so that you can access calls, music, navigation, and more.
